# Privacy Policy for ScanAces

**Last Updated: November 25, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to ScanAces ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: When you create an account, we collect your email address and password (encrypted)
- **User-Generated Content**: Photos you capture or select through the app

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Camera Data**: Photos taken using the in-app camera feature
- **Photo Library Access**: Photos you select from your device's photo library
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with the app
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers

### 1.3 Authentication Data

If you use third-party authentication (Google, Apple):
- Email address
- Name (if provided by the authentication service)
- Profile picture (if provided by the authentication service)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the ScanAces service
- Process and store photos you capture or upload
- Perform optical character recognition (OCR) on images
- Allow you to save, view, and manage your scanned documents
- Authenticate your identity and maintain your account security
- Improve our app's functionality and user experience
- Respond to your requests and provide customer support

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Storage

- Your photos and data are stored securely using Supabase, a third-party cloud service provider
- Photos are stored in your device's photo library if you grant permission
- Authentication credentials are encrypted and securely managed

### 3.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure authentication protocols
- Regular security updates and monitoring
- Access controls and authentication requirements

## 4. Permissions We Request

### 4.1 Camera Access

- **Why**: To capture photos of documents for scanning
- **When**: Only when you actively use the camera feature
- **Control**: You can revoke this permission in your device settings at any time

### 4.2 Photo Library Access

- **Why**: To save scanned documents and access existing photos for OCR processing
- **When**: When you choose to save or access photos
- **Control**: You can choose "Limited Access" to select specific photos, or revoke access entirely in device settings

### 4.3 Limited Photo Access (iOS)

- You can grant limited access to select only specific photos
- The app can only access photos you explicitly select
- You can add or remove photo access at any time using the "+" button in the gallery

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We do NOT:

- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your photos with advertisers
- Use your data for marketing purposes without consent
- Share your information except as described below

### We MAY share your information:

- **With Service Providers**: Supabase for data storage and authentication
- **For Legal Reasons**: If required by law, court order, or governmental request
- **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly authorize sharing
- **Business Transfers**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### 7.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your account and associated data
- Export your data
- Opt-out of optional data collection

### 7.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

- **Delete Account**: Contact us at info@qualaces.com
- **Access Data**: Request a copy of your data by emailing us
- **Revoke Permissions**: Use your device settings to revoke camera or photo access
- **Update Information**: Edit your profile within the app

## 8. Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: We retain your data while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Photos**: Photos remain in your device's photo library until you manually delete them
- **Backups**: Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days after deletion

## 9. Children's Privacy

ScanAces is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at info@qualaces.com.

## 10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. By using ScanAces, you consent to such transfers. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:
- Posting the new policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Sending an email notification (if applicable)

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
